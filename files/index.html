Our Story

Born from the
Ancient Art of Fire

Ember & Oak began as a small roadside grill in 2012, when founder Marco Renaldi dragged a century-old cast iron oven from his grandfather's farm in Tuscany and planted it in the heart of the city.

What started as weekend pop-ups grew into something bigger ‚Äî a gathering place where the ritual of cooking over real wood and open flame became the beating heart of every meal. Every log we burn is hand-selected white oak from local sustainable farms. Every ember tells a story.

Today, our kitchen is led by Head Chef Aria Chen, whose philosophy is simple: great fire, great ingredients, great people. We do not rush. We do not compromise. We cook the way fire demands ‚Äî slowly, honestly, beautifully.
